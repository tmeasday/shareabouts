About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of CrowdSpot - an intuitive map-based tool designed to complement the planning process for public space improvements. CrowdSpot is also great for research and knowledge sharing projects.

The easiest way to understand how CrowdSpot works it to try it out - it only takes a minute to. Follow these steps:

1. Add a spot - First choose your location by dragging the map under the marker.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.

Our demo has been setup to allow you to pin spots on locations where you want WiFi Hotspots, Bike Share Docks and Public Toilets - just three of hundreds of possible uses.
